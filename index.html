About

Inspired by the likes of King's Quest and Monkey Island, Cave Escape is an interactive text-based adventure game that puts the player into the shoes of an unnamed adventurer left to die if the depths of a massive cave system.


Make decisions based on the situation, and select what to do next by clicking a button after weighing your options.
